Scrambled Eggs

Thomas Keller's Scrambled Eggs Recipe

This recipe produces soft, luxurious scrambled eggs that are a welcome addition to any breakfast. Make sure you are using the freshest possible eggs as the quality of the eggs will determine the quality of the final product


Ingredients

  • 4 eggs

  • 1 teaspoon kosher salt

  • 2 tablespoons unsalted butter

  • 1 1/2 tablespoons crème fraîche

Steps

  1. In a small bowl, beat the eggs with the salt until they are homogenous.

  2. Heat a 2-quart (2-l) saucier over medium-low heat and add the butter.

  3. When the butter has melted, strain the beaten eggs through a sieve into the pan. Cook, continually whisking the eggs, until they thicken yet are still very creamy and not completely set.

  4. Whisk in the crème fraîche and cook for a few seconds longer. Spoon the eggs onto warm serving plates and serve immediately. Serves 2.
